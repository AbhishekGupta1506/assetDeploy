Steps:-

1.Goto SchedulerForBenita:createTasks service
2.Select pub.scheduler:addOneTimeTask and enter the date to a value in future and Time to a value in future
3.Select pub.scheduler:addRepeatingTask and enter the interval(You can retain 120 sec value if you want)
4.select pub.scheduler:addComplexTask and enter the start Date and time to current date.End Date and Time to Future
5.Run the service SchedulerForBenita:createTasks.

Scheduled tasks will be created.
